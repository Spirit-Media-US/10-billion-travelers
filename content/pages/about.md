### Extended Bio
Kevin White was born into a modest family in Western North Carolina with limited early travel experience until college. His first passport came at age 30. He has accumulated over 1 million miles across 26 countries. He has dual interests in mainstream destinations and remote exploration. Knowledgeable about travel industry operations. Offers vlogs, blogs, reviews, and consulting services.
