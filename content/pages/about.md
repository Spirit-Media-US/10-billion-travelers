"Born in a poor family in Western North Carolina, I had only traveled outside of NC for a few days until I went to college in South Carolina. I didn't even have a passport until I was 30 years old. Today, I've logged over 1 million miles and have traveled to 26 different countries around the world.

I love to travel. I love adventure. I love to travel to popular destinations but love even more to sleep under the stars where very few people have ever gone. I know how to travel. I know what makes airlines, hotels, restaurants, and attractions great! I'm blessed with a ton of practical travel experience and would love to share it with you.

Check out our Vlogs, Blogs, Reviews, and Travel Services. We will inspire & facilitate your travel dreams. Let us consult with you to make your hotel, restaurant, or attraction more profitable. Are your bags packed yet?"
