## Notes

- Very minimal design — no rounded corners, no shadows, no fancy effects
- Nav links are uppercase, small (12px), widely letter-spaced
- Buttons are outlined (white bg, teal border) not filled
- Color palette is teal + dark green/charcoal — nature/travel feel
- Google Fonts: Montserrat (headings/nav/buttons) + Lato (body)
